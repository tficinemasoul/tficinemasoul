TFI Cinema Soul is a free Telugu movie discovery platform. We take your privacy seriously and want to be transparent about how we handle information.

1. Information We Collect

TFI Cinema Soul does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use our service. However, we may collect limited, anonymous data through:

  • Usage Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (pages visited, time spent, device type). This data is anonymous and aggregated — we cannot identify individual users from it.
  • Chatbot Interactions: When you use the Tollywood Chatbot, your message is processed by Google's Gemini API to generate a response. We do not store your chat messages beyond the duration of your session.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies for analytics purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

2. How We Use Information

Any data we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve the performance and content of TFI Cinema Soul

3. Third-Party Services

TFI Cinema Soul integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • TMDB (The Movie Database): We use the TMDB API to fetch movie posters, backdrops, and cast photos. See TMDB Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API: Powers the Tollywood Chatbot. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage tracking. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • OTT Platforms (Aha, Netflix, Prime Video, etc.): When you click "Watch Now", you are redirected to these platforms. Their own privacy policies apply once you leave our site.

This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.

4. Data Security

TFI Cinema Soul is a static website hosted on Netlify. We do not maintain our own servers or store personal data. Your browsing on our site is protected by HTTPS encryption.
